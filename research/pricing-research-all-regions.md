## SUMMARY: PRICING VALIDATION

All of Dudley's pricing is **market-validated** and positions FinanceFlo.ai as a **premium boutique** consultancy:

1. **UK (GBP)**: All rates sit squarely in the mid-to-upper freelance range, well below agency rates. Competitive and defensible.

2. **EU (EUR)**: Hourly rates are at the premium end but justified by the triple specialization (AI + Finance + Sage Intacct). The audit range appears to have a typo (€5,000-€5,000 should likely be €5,000-€15,000). Senior consultant rate is same as consultant (€180) which may need differentiation.

3. **ZA (ZAR)**: Rates are premium for the SA market but significantly below UK/EU equivalents, making them attractive for SA-based companies. The audit and retainer ranges are well-positioned for the SA mid-market.

### Key Observations
- Finance sector commands 20-30% premium over general tech consulting
- AI specialization adds 40-60% premium over general IT consulting
- Sage Intacct niche further justifies premium positioning (limited supply)
- All pricing excludes VAT/Sales Tax as stated
- Pricing is indicative and project-based, not fixed (per user's knowledge base requirements)
