  - Excavators

  - Trenching equipment
  - TLBs (Tractor Loader Backhoes)

- **Availability:** 24/7 service

### 2. Demolition Services
- **Certification:** Level 4 SE PE Demolition Grading
- **Specialization:** Specialized demolition and dismantling projects
- **Recent Projects:** Bayworld Demolition, Swartkops Powerstation Demolition, Substation Demolish

### 3. Earthworks & Excavation
- **Services:**
  - Precision grading and earthworks
  - Land preparation for development

  - Road construction support

### 4. Excavation Subcontracting

- Project-based work from greenfield to brownfield

## Current Technology Stack

### Accounting System
- **Primary:** Sage Pastel Partner Version 19 (desktop)
- **Configuration:** 10 companies on Pastel, 2 on spreadsheets/outsourced
- **Database:** Pervasive database (legacy)
- **Challenges:**
  - Data integrity issues in larger companies
  - Multiple database instances (one per company)
  - Year-end database proliferation
  - Database size limitations (~3-4GB causing errors)
  - Difficulty accessing historical data
- **Migration Plans:** Planning to move to Sage One (cloud) by end of March 2026
  - IT team currently testing integration with Whimbrel

### Operations Management System
- **System Name:** Whimbrel
- **Developer:** Sugar Software (Johannesburg)
- **Type:** Custom-built, cloud-based operational system
- **Development Timeline:** ~8 years of development
- **Technology:** Spreadsheet-driven (likely Microsoft Access-based)
- **Cloud Platform:** Microsoft Azure SQL Server databases
- **Status:** Complete and operational, but continuous improvements

**Whimbrel Capabilities:**
- Hours capture and tracking
- Machine/equipment management
- Operator assignment and tracking
- Rate management (wet rate vs. dry rate)
- Workshop management
- Job costing
- Stock management (limited inventory)
- Invoicing (primary invoice generation)
- Mobile access for directors
- Integration with Pastel (one-way data push)

**Whimbrel Importance:**
- Non-negotiable system (directors are committed)
- Core to operations
- All operational data captured in Whimbrel
- Pastel used primarily for financial records and compliance

### Other Systems
- **Aircraft Maintenance:** Paper-based service records at hangar
- **Job Cards:** Manual, paper-heavy process
- **Communication:** WhatsApp, email, text messaging
- **Estimations/Quotes:** Manual process, spreadsheets

## Business Model & Pricing

### Pricing Structure
- **Base Rate:** Per hour, per machine
- **Transport:** Charged separately (to/from site)
- **Fuel:**
  - **Wet Rate:** Within 50km radius - company provides fuel
  - **Dry Rate:** Beyond 50km - client provides fuel
- **Ground Engaging Tools (GET):** Wear items charged to customers
- **Winter Dry Rain:** Special rate structure

### Sales Process
- **No dedicated sales team:** Operations staff network while working
- **Word of mouth:** Strong reputation in industry
- **Customer-initiated:** Clients typically tell them what equipment is needed
- **Quotation Process:** Site manager and directors provide pricing based on customer and project

### Project Types
1. **Simple Hire:** Single machine, short-term, local
2. **Complex Projects:** Multiple machines, 3+ months, turnkey delivery
3. **Subcontracting:** Work for major construction firms
4. **Development Preparation:** Land leveling, trenching, site prep for construction

## Maintenance & Operations

### Workshop Operations
- **Location:** On-site workshop
- **Team:** Multiple technicians and mechanics
- **Services:**
  - Preventive maintenance
  - Repairs and breakdowns
  - On-site service (minimize machine downtime)
  - In-workshop repairs for major work

### Inventory Management
- **Philosophy:** Just-in-time, minimal inventory
- **Items Held:** Basic oils, filters (low quantities)
- **Tracking:** Maintained in Whimbrel, not in Pastel
- **Procurement:** Order parts as needed

### Aircraft Maintenance
- **Service Records:** Paper-based at hangar
- **Compliance:** Annual services, CAA service bulletins
- **Clients:** Own aircraft + sold aircraft (ongoing maintenance contracts)
- **Staff:** Older mechanics, less tech-savvy
- **Challenge:** Paper-heavy, manual job sheets

## Pain Points & Opportunities

### Identified Challenges
1. **Data Integrity Issues:** Pastel database corruption in larger companies
2. **Multi-Company Complexity:** 10+ separate Pastel instances
3. **Historical Data Access:** Difficult to retrieve old financial data
4. **Paper-Heavy Processes:** Aircraft maintenance records
5. **Manual Job Cards:** Hangar operations still on paper
6. **System Fragmentation:** Multiple systems, spreadsheets, communication tools
7. **Limited Visibility:** Difficult to get consolidated group reporting
8. **Change Management:** Older staff resistant to new technology

### Quick Win Opportunities
1. **Multi-Company Financial System:** Single cloud-based system for all entities
2. **Aircraft Job Card Digitization:** Mobile app for mechanics
3. **Service Record Digitization:** Convert paper records to digital system
4. **Consolidated Reporting:** Group-level financial visibility
5. **AI-Powered Insights:** Predictive maintenance, profit margin analysis
6. **Automated Workflows:** Reduce manual data entry and errors
