# Design Reference Notes — Proposal Websites

## DanGeeCarken.financeflo.ai
- Landing page with hero section: dark navy/teal background, office/tech imagery overlay
- Two proposal options side by side (Recommended vs Full)
- Each option card has: title, description, bullet points with check icons, price, timeline, CTA button
- Recommended option highlighted with teal accent and "RECOMMENDED" badge
- Footer: ERP Solutions branding, Quick Links, Contact info
- Sticky header with company name + "Schedule Call" CTA
- Color scheme: dark navy background, teal accents, white text
- Typography: serif headings (italic), sans-serif body
- Clean, professional, corporate feel
- ZAR pricing (R597K, R1.28M)
- Footer links: Simplified Proposal, Full Proposal, ROI Calculator, Implementation, Downloads

## HNP Boerdery.financeflo.ai
- Hero section with farm/business aerial photo, light overlay
- Badge: "HNP Boerdery — Odoo ERP Proposal"
- Two proposal cards side by side: Foundation First (recommended) vs Full Transformation
- Each card has: Year 1 Investment price, weeks, users, companies count
- Detailed includes list + Key Benefits list
- "Why Two Options?" section with 3 value props (Low-Risk Entry, Prove Before Scale, Clear Upgrade Path)
- Testimonial section with quote from Agricultural CFO
- Footer: Q&A ERP Solutions branding, proposal validity date
- Color scheme: dark green (#1a4a3a), cream/warm white background, green accents
- Language toggle (EN)
- Much more detailed than DanGeeCarken — multi-section scrolling page
- ZAR pricing throughout
