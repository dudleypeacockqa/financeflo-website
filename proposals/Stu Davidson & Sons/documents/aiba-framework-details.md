# AIBA Framework Details (Extracted from PPTX + Video)

## What is AIBA?
AIBA = AI Business Analysis. A consulting framework designed to pinpoint areas within a business where AI or automation can significantly enhance efficiency and effectiveness. It systematically identifies opportunities for integrating these technologies to streamline processes, improve decision-making, and boost overall business performance.

## Five Key Pillars
1. **Operational Efficiency** — Unlocks significant productivity gains and enhances operational efficiencies
2. **Competitive Edge** — Early AI adoption gives your business a definitive edge in the market
3. **Customization** — Crafts AI solutions that are perfectly aligned with unique business challenges and goals
4. **Scalability** — Ensures AI strategies scale with your business, supporting sustainable growth
5. **Survival of Fittest** — Businesses that don't adapt to technology changes don't survive

## Three-Phase Process

### Phase 1: Preliminary Assessment (Green boxes)
- Initial Meeting with Prospect
  - Company Overview and AI Objectives
  - Processes, Functions and Org Structure
  - Bottom Up Questions (People & Culture)
  - Top Down Questions (Leadership & Strategy)
- Preliminary Assessment and Proposal

### Phase 2: AIBA Framework (Blue boxes) — PAID
**Step 2: Gather + Train** (Week 1-3)
- Kick-off Meeting
- Baseline Questionnaire
- Tech Stack Review
- Company History, Objectives and Pain Points
- Training Session 1
- Training Session 2

**Step 3: Understand** (Week 4-5, then Week 6-8)
Deep dives into departments:
- Leadership and Strategy
- Customer Service
- Marketing
- Sales/Business Development
- Finance/Accounting
- Market and Customer
- Supply Chain and Logistics
- Human Resources
- Procurement
- Legal

**Step 4: Strategy & Alignment** (Week 8-10)
- Assessments and Strategic Recommendations
- Presentation, Project Prioritization and Alignment
- Project Timeline & Budget Breakdown
- Quarterly Outline and Initial 12 Week Action Plan + Advisory & Services Proposal
- Next Steps

### Phase 3: Advisory & Ongoing Project
- FCAIO (Fractional Chief AI Officer) Engagement — ongoing
- Project-based AI Implementations: Project 1, Project 2, Project N — ongoing

## Timeline and Key Stages
- Week 1: Kick-Off Call, Create Project Plan in PM Tool
- Week 2: Training Session 1
- Week 3: Training Session 2, Task Mapping (identify repetitive tasks, identify where AI could be plugged in)
- Week 4: Deep Dive 1
- Week 5: Deep Dive 2
- Week 6: Deep Dive 3
- Week 7: Deep Dive 4
- Week 8-9: AIBA Final Report Preparation
- Week 10: Deliver the AIBA Report and Present
- Week 11+: Ongoing Advisory and Implementation
